Angelic Love

I created this sketch while I was out of state painting the backside of a Lowes for a contract paint company. The men I drove to the job wanted to go to a gentleman's club after we were done with the job and one of the dancer's inspired me to draw this. The AI version is the complete scene of what I saw which was her soul, and the background represents the place we both where in. Even in the darkest of places beauty can shine bright : )
