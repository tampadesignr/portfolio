Big Bang Comedy Tour

 

The concept for this logo was to create the energy and feel of big and showing a bang and the typical bomb explosion cloud around a microphone I feel sells the idea, while relating to the mental visual picture created by this combination of words and imagery. This logo I used to test a few styles changes to see how AI handles this kind of imagery. Then I used AI to apply the logo concept to a mock-up location. I did not try to refine the logo with these images, I was more interested in AI ability to apply styles to words and imagery.
