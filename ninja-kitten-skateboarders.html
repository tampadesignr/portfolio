Ninja Kitten Skateboarders

I spent over 20 years with the mind of a skateboarder, every place you go, you are looking for something to skate. I would still be searching for places to skate if healing from falls didn't take 10 times longer now : ) . I drew this a long time ago learning to draw the world around me. The first thing I thought when I saw this sketch, was a kitten ninja doing a trick going down the staircase, and naturally made the scene a bit funnier with some fun prompting.
