Never forgotten

This was a rough sketch for a concept I had to honor the fallen hero's, a beach of dreams unseen by the living world. A place where the hero's watch over the living world. A place that lives in the mind of those who live because hero's give their life to save other no matter the cost. The buildings live on in spirit preserved by the fallen who live on in our memories. I never had time to draw this concept and it was a perfect candidate to see what AI could do with my rough sketch.
