Groovy City

There are a few thing I love to draw and silly, funky, groovy cities is one of them. this was a rough idea for a city that featured basic shapes to convey a fun playful city. Sometimes I like to lightly draw an idea and then use a marker or something with a thicker line weight to see the concept from a distance, stepping back and imagining the concept from a distance sometimes I can see a direction to further the concept. For the AI image made from this sketch the first thing that came to mind was something whimsical to drive home the concept of a magical fun city.
