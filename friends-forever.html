Friends Forever

This is a not the best version of this drawing I made a long time ago, the digital copy has a lot of extra noise, but you can see my style, I added a touch of whimsy to the picture I drew this portrait of a friend and her dog. The color versions are the AI versions I made while seeing how much additional whimsy I could add while maintaining the original black and white drawing style. I also experimented with extending the original image by making the size of the AI image larger then the input.
