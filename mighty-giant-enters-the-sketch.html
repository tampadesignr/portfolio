Mighty Giant Enters the Sketch

When i was learning to draw the world around me, I drew everything. This is an unfinished study of a SUV on the street of my dorm in Manhattan at F.I.T college. My perspective is off on the car view that is why you keep drawing and strengthening your ability to translate the world onto paper. I used this sketch with AI testing its ability to tell a story around my original elements of the drawing. For this test of AI my first thought was to test something AI struggled to create which was the concept of just seeing a giant's feet. Once I had the feet, something was missing, so i added some characters to the concept, which then lead to the final idea to have them looking up to tie all elements together into a cohesive story.
