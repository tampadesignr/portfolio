Baby Boomer

This was a project from when I went back to school for interactive media and design. The concept for this colored pencil drawing was meant to show the meeting of life and death, using iconic symbology to create a thought stimulating scene. Some elements of the scene where added to allow the mind to run free and allow the viewer to question the thoughts in their mind of what they are seeing.
